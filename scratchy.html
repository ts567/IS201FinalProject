A Bit About Ranching

By Ty Smith

So, I love ranching. I grew up all around animals.

Growing up, my family owned many different animals inlcuding: horses, goats, dogs, cats, chickens, donkeys, rabbits, and much more. Some of my favorite activites, chores, memories, etc. have been with animals.

When I was 15, my sister ended up marrying a rancher and that opened up even more opportunies to be around animals. At 16 I then ended up working for my brother-in-law and was able to do so many things that I really enjoyed. Some of the things I was able to do based on seasons were:

  1. Summer
    • Fixing fences
    • Spraying weeds
    • Working calfs
  2. Fall
    • Gather cows horseback
    • Sort cows
  3. Winter
    • Calf cows
    • Feed
  4. Spring
    • Put cows in summer pasture
    • Vacinate and work cows

^Me moving cows to different watering hole during the summer wiht my brother-in-law
